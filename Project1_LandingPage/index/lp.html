Clairo.

Claire Elizabeth Cottrill (born August 18, 1998), known professionally as Clairo, is an American singer-songwriter. Clairo rose to prominence following the viral success of the music video for her lo-fi single "Pretty Girl" in 2017.

In a 2015 interview with Rookie, she recalled of this first recording, "It took me hours to figure out if I wanted to post it on Facebook or not to even tell my friends that I'd made it. I was so nervous about it!"

Latest News

  • In January 2024, Clairo teased a new album with a post on Instagram, captioning it "maybe this year".
  • On May 23, Clairo announced her third album, titled Charm.
